■ About This Document
────────────────

This document is a personal research memo focused on:

・equipment inheritance
・arrange inheritance
・auto-arrange behavior
・self-inheritance contamination
・recursive inheritance
・selection / lottery processing
・success rate models

in Rune Factory 4 Special and Rune Factory 5.

The material is based on:

・in-game testing
・observation
・theoretical analysis
・practical gameplay experimentation

and was originally created while attempting to optimize villager equipment setups in RF5 while preserving roleplay consistency, character identity, and overall world coherence.

During this process, multiple undocumented or poorly understood inheritance behaviors were discovered, eventually leading to a much larger-scale observation and organization project.

────────────────

“best-in-slot equipment guide.”

・real gameplay usability
・long play sessions
・villager equipment optimization
・mass-production feasibility
・cognitive load management
・visual readability
・crafting difficulty
・inheritance success rates
・reproducibility
・roleplay considerations
・world / lore consistency

Because of this, some builds intentionally prioritize:

・stability
・scalability
・maintainability
・crafting consistency
・practical usability

over:

・theoretical maximum performance
・mathematically optimal configurations

Villager equipment design also considers:

・combat performance
・elemental resistance

・visual distinguishability
・character identity
・world consistency
・roleplay coherence

■ About the Scale of This Project
────────────────

What originally began as a relatively small inheritance memo gradually escalated into an unexpectedly massive research document.

Even the author cannot fully explain why the scope expanded this far.

At present, the single greatest unresolved issue may not be the Rune Factory inheritance system itself, but rather the author's inability to stop falling deeper into inheritance-mechanics rabbit holes once curiosity is triggered.

■ Final Notes
────────────────

The inheritance systems in the Rune Factory series are extremely complex and still contain many unresolved behaviors.

The author is also aware that this project may appear somewhat excessive, strange, or dangerously overengineered for what originally started as a simple inheritance memo.

However, because similarly structured large-scale public research materials for Rune Factory inheritance systems appear to be relatively rare, the author ultimately decided that releasing this “Object X” into the world would likely be more useful than leaving it permanently buried.

Hopefully, this document can serve as a useful reference for:

・future testing
・research
・optimization studies
・gameplay analysis
・community discussion
・preservation of knowledge surrounding these systems
