Efficient Friendship Optimization

- Favorite, Loved, and Neutral gifts appear to be evaluated independently based on repeated observations.
- Handmade gifts provide an additional friendship bonus.
- Therefore, preparing low-cost handmade gifts for daily distribution can be an efficient long-term strategy.
- Stable access to frequently used crops and flowers through shop inventory management may reduce preparation time.

Note:
These observations are based on repeated gameplay testing and should be regarded as practical long-term strategies rather than confirmed internal mechanics.
