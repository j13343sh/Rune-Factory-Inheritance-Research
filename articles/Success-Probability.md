# Success Probability

## Overview

Success Probability is an observation-based research topic describing how inheritance success may be interpreted through candidate generation and candidate count.

This article summarizes one conceptual interpretation derived from repeated gameplay observations and mathematical modeling.

---

## Why It Matters

Many inheritance outcomes appear random when viewed through individual gameplay observations.

However, observation results suggest that inheritance success probability may be interpreted through changes in Candidate Count generated during inheritance processing.

The Success Probability model should be interpreted as one branch of the broader Candidate Count Model.
